I’m Kelly a sophomore studying Computer Science at the University of Texas at Dallas.

I’ve always been passionate about design even since I was a young. Instead of dropping me off at daycare my parents would drop me off at an art studio where would learn how to draw on walls and other unconventional surfaces.

During quarantine like many of us I decided to learn a new skill and so I picked up coding. I fell in love with code after printing my first “hello world”, leading me to pursue a degree in CS at UTD. Here I hope to continue learning how to further my coding skills, and combine them with my passion for art.
